Aerial Analytics Solutions

Precision Insights from Above for Smarter Decision-Making

UBBIM’s Aerial Analytics Solutions harness the power of drone technology to deliver precise, real-time data for your projects. Whether it's land surveying, site monitoring, or infrastructure inspection, our solutions provide actionable insights that drive smarter, faster decision-making.

Our Services

Land Surveying

High-resolution aerial surveys that provide accurate topographical data, saving time and reducing costs compared to traditional methods.

Construction Site Monitoring

Regular aerial monitoring of construction sites to track progress, identify potential issues, and ensure compliance with project timelines

Aerial Mapping

Create precise, up-to-date maps for urban planning, environmental monitoring, and more using advanced drone technology.

Volume Calculation

Accurately calculate volumes of stockpiles, earthworks, and other materials using aerial data, ensuring precise resource management.

Key Benefits

Enhanced Accuracy

Capture precise, high-resolution data from above, ensuring every detail is accurately documented for informed decision-making.

Faster Project Turnaround

Accelerate project timelines with rapid data collection and real-time insights, allowing for quicker analysis and action.

Cost-Effective Data Collection

Reduce costs by leveraging drone technology for efficient data gathering, minimizing the need for manual labor and extensive equipment.

Improved Safety

Conduct inspections and surveys from a safe distance, reducing the risk to personnel and ensuring compliance with safety standards.
